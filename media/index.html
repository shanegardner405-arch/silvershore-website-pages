Company Boilerplate

SilverShore Partners is a growth consulting and M&A advisory firm serving owner-led service businesses in the lower middle market ($1M-$25M EBITDA). The firm helps business owners build revenue infrastructure, deploy custom AI Operating Systems, and prepare for successful exits while connecting qualified investors with proprietary off-market deal flow.

Founded by Shane Gardner, SilverShore operates at the intersection of growth consulting and transaction readiness, providing practical systems that increase enterprise value for owners planning to grow or exit within the next 1-5 years. The firm maintains a proprietary database of over 21,000 verified business owner records and works with PE firms, family offices, and search funds seeking off-market acquisition opportunities.

Shane Gardner

Founder, SilverShore Partners

Shane Gardner is the founder of SilverShore Partners, a growth consulting and M&A advisory firm focused on owner-led service businesses in the lower middle market. He works with business owners preparing to grow or exit and with institutional investors seeking off-market acquisition opportunities.

Before founding SilverShore, Shane built expertise across business development, AIOS operations, and M&A advisory. His background spans revenue infrastructure design, custom AI Operating Systems, and transaction support for businesses in the $1M-$25M EBITDA range.

Shane writes regularly about lower middle market M&A dynamics, growth strategy for service businesses, and the operational changes that drive enterprise value. His work is available at SilverShore Insights.
