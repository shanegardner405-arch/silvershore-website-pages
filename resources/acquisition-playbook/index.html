What Sets This Apart

Built for discipline, not speed

Most acquisition guides focus on getting deals done. This playbook focuses on getting the right deals done - and walking away from the wrong ones early.

Clear Go/No-Go Gates

Decision checkpoints after each phase to protect your capital and keep deals moving only when they should. Kill bad deals early.

Seller-Friendly Approach

Structure deals and terms that work for sellers while protecting your interests. Win deals without burning bridges or creating adversarial dynamics.

Post-Close Readiness

Day 1 integration plans to stabilize operations and start building value from the moment you close. Don't lose momentum after signing.

How to Use This Playbook

Get the most out of every module

1

Start at Your Stage

Find the module that matches where you are in your acquisition process. Each module builds on the previous one but can also stand alone.

2

Use the Checklists

Each module includes detailed checklists and frameworks. Use them to ensure you're covering all critical items at each stage.

3

Apply the Decision Gates

Follow the go/no-go criteria at each checkpoint. These gates protect you from continuing with deals that should be killed early.

4

Reference During Negotiations

Keep the playbook handy during deal discussions. The structuring and negotiation frameworks help you respond to proposals with discipline.

The Process

Five complete modules from thesis to outreach

1

Sourcing & Evaluation

Market mappingTarget criteriaFirst-pass screening

Market analysis, target identification, and first-pass evaluation framework. Understand the market, find the right targets, and know what to look for before investing significant time.

2

LOI & Initial Diligence

LOI strategyDiligence planningRisk identification

Letter of intent strategy, diligence planning, and what to expect in the first phase. Move efficiently without rushing critical review items that protect your downside.

3

Deep Diligence & Underwriting

QoE analysisOperational reviewLegal exposure

Financial, operational, legal, and customer diligence. Structured approach to catch issues before you are deep in the deal and committed to closing.

4

Deal Structure & Negotiation

StructuringEarnoutsNegotiation tactics

Price structure, terms, conditions, and negotiation strategy. Only negotiate what moves price, risk, or control - everything else is noise.

5

Post-Close Integration

Day 1 plan100-day roadmapValue creation

Day 1 readiness, 100-day plan, and stabilization strategy. Take over clean and start building value immediately without losing momentum or key people.
